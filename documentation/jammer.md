# Jammer Overview

The jammer is the source of interference in the wireless environment. It switches between idle and active states, and when active it operates at one of three power levels.

In this project, the jammer is important for both challenge and opportunity: it reduces communication quality, but it also creates conditions for backscatter transmission and energy harvesting.

By learning the jammer pattern, the RL agent can choose actions that maintain throughput and adapt to adversarial conditions.
